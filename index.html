About Me

Hi! My name is Supryadi, a 3rd year PhD student at Tianjin University, China. I'm a member of the TJUNLP Lab at Tianjin University, where our research focus on Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Large Language Models (LLMs). I'm advised by Prof. Deyi Xiong.

My research centers on multilingual LLMs, low-resource LLMs, and LLMs evaluation.
